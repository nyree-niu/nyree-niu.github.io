1. Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people

Understanding neuroplasticity benefits people by showing them that their brains can be reshaped through consistent practice and challenges. Consistent practices and challeneges can enhanding the neural pathway It provides a scientific foundation for building the mindset that skills can be developed through focus, regular practice, and resilience.



2.Discuss how you might engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your benefit

Practice makes perfect and permanent: This applies to all types of learning. With limited study time, it’s important to focus on quality and consistency in every study session.

Concentration: Stay focused on what I’m doing by putting my phone away and studying in a quiet, distraction-free environment.

Review: Regularly revisit what I’ve learned to strengthen memory and understanding.

Research and note-taking: Learning is a lifelong journey, and questions will always arise, such as understanding a piece of code or a new concept. Luckly there are many resources available to support learning. Taking notes and reflecting on research helps deepen comprehension and retention.
