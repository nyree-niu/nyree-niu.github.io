    2. Describe how your culture and the people around you have influenced your values and identity.

    I grew up in China, where the culture is very different from New Zealand. In China, the culture is more collectivist, where group harmony, family reputation, and community interests often come before individual desires. In New Zealand, the culture is more individualistic, where personal freedom, independence, and self-expression are strongly valued.

    I have been influenced by both cultures. I value family and community, but I also value personal freedom and independence. I respect personal choices on what they believe and what they do.
