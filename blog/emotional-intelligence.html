Emotional Intelligence

24-08-2025

    1. What is Emotional Intelligence?

    Emotional Intelligence (EQ) is the ability to perceive, understand, use and manage your own emotions and the emotions of others in postive ways.


    2. How is EQ different to IQ?

    They are two different ways of being smart. IQ is "book smart", measuring cognitive abilities and logical reasoning, while EQ is "people smart" measuring ability to handle social complexities, motivate and connect with people.


    3. Why is EQ important?

    High EQ is important in almost every area of life:

    In workplace, people with high EQ can handle stress better, communicate more effectively, and resolve confict more smoothly.

    In personal life, high EQ can help manage stress healthily and lead to greater self-confidence, resilience, and overall life satisfaction. High EQ allows for clear communication, empathy, and healthy conflict resolution with family and friends.
